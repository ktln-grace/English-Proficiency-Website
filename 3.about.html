About the Quiz Masters

Meet the quiz masters of this educational website

Mission

Quizzy aims to make every individual competent in English through many materials, interactive, curated, and collaborative tools for learning, and modern mode of guidance to achieve their goals through confidence building and competence nurturing.

Vision

Quizzy envisions developing strong, positive bonds with students, encouraging them to pursue long-term independence in learning, develop confidence beginning in proficiency, and gaining lasting academic knowledge; thus creating individuals who are well-rounded, and can easily adapt in a rapidly changing post-modern globalized environment.
