Directions

Welcome to the English Proficiency Quiz! Before you begin, please read the following directions carefully to ensure a smooth and successful quiz experience:

1. Prepare Yourself: Take a moment to get comfortable and mentally prepared for the quiz. Find a quiet space where you can focus without distractions.

2. Check Your Internet Connection: Ensure that you have a stable internet connection to prevent any interruptions during the quiz.

3. Review the Topic: Take a moment to review relevant information beforehand. This can help refresh your memory and boost your confidence going into the quiz.

4. Eliminate Distractions: Minimize distractions by silencing your phone, closing unnecessary tabs or applications, and letting others know you're about to take a quiz.

5. Double-Check: Finally, double-check that you're ready to begin the quiz. Once you click the "Start Quiz" button, the adventure begins!
